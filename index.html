Opportunity

In the past 3 years, Online Shopping has flourished in a Country where the GDP recently fell from 4.8 to 3.69 and yet falling day after day. While websites like Myntra and Jabong remain the major players of the Market and advertise themselves to the Indian Sentimental Economy encouraging people to spend more and more on luxury items derailing the conventional markets and promoting the virtual market place. These Start-ups have exponentially manifold their profit turnover just in 2-3 years comprising of Millions of User-base at a time. On the other hand a Social Networking platform has emerged in the recent years which has also increased its User-base exponentially from 1000 5 years back to 1 Billion at present: Facebook. Since Facebook achieved its dominant part of the traffic from India and even though Shopping giants advertise various discounts on friend referral signup via Facebook, the system does not really turn out to be a profitable one neither helping out the firm neither the Users since the Firms use tacky financial and technical bounds before User could really avail to those discounts. There is need for a system which could take the best of both Shopping and Socializing Platform in a country comprising of 1.27Billion population where people are more social compared to other countries while bringing out the best of downtrodden crisis that the economy of India face today (Consistent decline of GDP).

Solution

Project CSSP does just that using the best of both worlds where the tradition of gifting comes to life in a way rejoicing as well adaptive to Indian psychology. It imports user’s cart from just any shopping site, converts it into a wish list to be displayed on user’s wall and broadcasts the wish list to all of user’s friends which are indeed invited from Facebook. The gifting system awards points equivalent to amount gifted and in return receives discount coupons which can be used on supported sites and retail outlets. Friends can also collect points and redeem them for a bigger coupon accordingly. User receives gift amount from friends which can then be used to fulfil the wish list. The amount equivalent to the gifting amount is deducted from the person gifting mobile balance using in-app purchase feature which removes the hassle for Credit/Debit card payment system and make gifting a breeze.
